# **App Name**: Pedido InteliPreço

## Core Features:

- XLSX Price Sheet Upload: Allows authenticated users to upload their XLSX price list files to initiate data processing.
- AI-Powered Price Sheet Processing: Utilizes an AI tool to intelligently parse the uploaded XLSX file, identifying product names, units, and pricing (closed/fractional load), as well as discount structures from multiple sections and factory-specific sheets.
- Product and Discount Database: Stores all extracted product details (name, unit, prices per factory, etc.) and discount information in a database for persistent storage and rapid access.
- Interactive Product Catalog: Displays an up-to-date catalog of all products with their corresponding prices, enabling users to view, search, and filter items by factory or product name.
- Dynamic Discount Calculation: Applies specified discounts from the database to product prices in real-time, reflecting adjusted costs during the order creation process.
- Order Assembly Interface: Provides a user-friendly interface to build new orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and reviewing dynamically calculated totals incorporating all relevant prices and discounts.

## Style Guidelines:

- The app uses a light color scheme inspired by clarity and professionalism. The primary color, a calming blue, is #4582A1, offering a professional yet inviting feel. The background color, a very light, almost white blue, is #F0F3F4, ensuring readability and freshness. An accent green, #70D65C, is used for highlights and interactive elements, symbolizing freshness and efficiency.
- All text within the application, including headlines and body text, will use the 'Inter' sans-serif font, chosen for its modern, legible, and objective characteristics, ensuring clarity in displaying product and price data.
- Employ a set of clean, minimalist line icons. Icons should clearly convey their function without clutter, maintaining the app's focus on data and workflow efficiency.
- The layout prioritizes clarity and organization, using ample whitespace and distinct sections for data tables, forms, and navigation. Information hierarchy will be emphasized to guide users through pricing updates and order creation workflows.
- Subtle, non-intrusive animations and transitions will be used to provide visual feedback for user actions, such as confirming file uploads, data processing completion, or successful addition of items to an order, enhancing the user experience without distraction.
